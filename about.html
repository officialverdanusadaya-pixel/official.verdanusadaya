About Verda Nusadaya

A trusted agro-industry company committed to delivering premium products to the global market with the highest quality standards.

PT Verda Nusadaya: PT Verda Nusadaya: Premium Agro Commodities

PT Verda Nusadaya is a leading exporter and trader of high-quality agricultural products from Indonesia. Based in North Lombok, West Nusa Tenggara, we are committed to delivering the finest commodities sourced directly from trusted local farmers.

With a strong focus on quality, consistency, and sustainability, we build long-term partnerships both domestically and internationally. Each product undergoes rigorous selection and quality control to meet global standards, ensuring safe and premium commodities for clients worldwide.

We believe in Indonesia’s abundant natural resources and are dedicated to enhancing the value of agricultural products, empowering farmers, and providing competitive solutions for the global market.
