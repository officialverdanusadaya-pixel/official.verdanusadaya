About Verda Nusadaya

A trusted agro-industry company committed to delivering premium products to the global market with the highest quality standards.

PT Verda Nusadaya Agro: Premium Agro Commodities

PT Verda Nusadaya Agro is a company engaged in the trade and export of Indonesia’s finest agricultural products. Based in North Lombok, West Nusa Tenggara, we are committed to delivering premium agro commodities sourced directly from trusted local farmers.

With a strong focus on quality, consistency, and sustainability, PT Verda Nusadaya Agro continues to build long-term partnerships both domestically and internationally. Every product we deliver undergoes strict selection and quality control processes to meet global standards.

We believe Indonesia’s strength lies in the richness of its natural resources. Therefore, we are dedicated to enhancing the value of agricultural commodities, empowering farmers, and providing products that are safe, high-quality, and globally competitive.

Vision & Mission

Our vision and commitment to advancing Indonesia's agroindustry sector.

Our Vision

To become a leading agroindustry company that brings Indonesia’s agricultural commodities to the global market through premium quality, sustainability, and long-term partnerships with local farmers.

Our Mission

  • To provide premium-quality agricultural commodities that meet global standards.
  • To build a transparent, efficient, and sustainable supply chain in collaboration with local farmers.
  • To empower farmers through training, guidance, and value enhancement for their products.
  • To expand international trade networks and bring Indonesia’s agricultural commodities to the global market.

Company Values

The core principles that guide every step we take in delivering premium agricultural commodities with quality, integrity, and sustainability.

Innovation

Developing creative solutions in processing, packaging, and distribution to meet the demands of the global agricultural market.

Integrity

Upholding honesty, transparency, and commitment in every business relationship, from local farmers to international clients.

Quality

Ensuring every commodity undergoes strict selection and quality control processes to meet global industry standards.

Collaboration

Prioritizing teamwork with farmers, business partners, and internal teams to build a strong supply chain and create positive impact for all stakeholders.
